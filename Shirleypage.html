Shirley

★★★☆☆

I've been a shopper for a while. Overall the shop is decent but personally I think the quality and service can be improve. I'm happy to give them credit for their excellent selection of accessories. They genuinely offer some trendy and interesting pieces, and the price are affordable. This is great stop if you purely for variety on budget.

However, the reason I'm rating 3 stars is the inconsistency in quality. While some of the items I had brought had helf up wonderfully, a few others specifically the strap of the autumn bracelet broke much sooner than I expected. I understand affordable pricing sometimes means compromises, but I prefer a little more durability across the board. If the quality control are better, definetely it will be a good 4 or 5 star store. I'll continue to shop here but definetely need to pay more attention to the quality before buying.
